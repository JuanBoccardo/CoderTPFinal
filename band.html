The Band

Band's lead singer

Band's lead singer, Singer Voice, was born in 1996 in Italy. From a young age he started singing, and later in his teens he starter pursuing a career as a vocalist.

Band's bassist

Band's bassist, String Bass, was born in 2001 in Italy. Once a poor kid on the streets, she met someone who played for money in pubs. He would then become her mentor, teaching her the basics of playing Bass. After her success, she visited her mentor regularly.

Band's drummer

Band's drummer, Mike, is the oddest of Band's member. His birth date is unclear, however, it is speculated that he was born around 1990. He learned to play the drums a few days before joining the band, when he interrupted a private recording, saying he was bored.

Band's guitarist

Band's guitarist, Strato Paul, was born in 1994 in London. Following his teenage dream as a guitarist, he put the band together and managed to record the first band's album.
